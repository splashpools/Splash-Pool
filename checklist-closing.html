Pool Closing Checklist

Time to close your pool for the winter! As your pool closing is scheduled, please help us following the next steps.

Important Steps

  • Water Balance: Understanding we’re at the end of the pool season, we assume your pool water is balanced! Please specify whether you’d like us to balance the chemicals prior to closing it.
  • Debris Removal: A fair amount of leaves are removed from top and bottom of your pool (15 min) included with your service. If this amount is excessive, additional fees will apply.
  • Access & Clear Area: Please make sure the filter area and any other pool related areas are clear from overgrown plants, shrubs and/or yard objects to guarantee access and be able to perform a full closing and avoid trip back charges.
  • Closing Goods: Have all pool closing goods nearby or accessible for us (Pool winter cover, water bags, etc).
  • Pool Side Hardware: To avoid damages to your premises (garages, sheds or others) all pool side hardware will be set pool side or near a specific place for you to organize them to your specific needs and liking.
